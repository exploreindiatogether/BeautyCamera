# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# Beauty Camera App – Version 1.0 (MVP)

## 1. Overview

### Product Name

Beauty Camera (Working Title)

### Version

1.0 (MVP)

### Objective

Build a fast, lightweight Android beauty camera application that allows users to capture selfies with real-time beauty effects and filters, edit photos, and share them easily.

The MVP should validate market demand while keeping development complexity and cost low.

---

# 2. Goals

## Primary Goals

* Launch on Google Play as quickly as possible.
* Provide a smooth real-time beauty camera experience.
* Offer essential beauty enhancements.
* Support editing of captured and existing photos.
* Build a solid technical foundation for future AR and AI features.

## Non-Goals

The following are intentionally excluded from V1.0:

* Video recording
* AR makeup
* AI retouch
* User accounts
* Cloud synchronization
* Social community
* Premium subscriptions
* Livestream support

---

# 5. Core Features

## 5.1 Camera Module

### Functional Requirements

* Launch directly into camera preview.
* Support front camera.
* Support rear camera.
* Switch camera.
* Flash (rear camera).
* Capture photo.
* Countdown timer:

  * Off
  * 3 seconds
  * 5 seconds
  * 10 seconds
* Display optional rule-of-thirds grid.

### Acceptance Criteria

* Camera opens within 2 seconds.
* Capture latency under 300 ms.
* Preview runs at 30 FPS or higher on supported devices.
